# Screenshots — beta.15

> Independent open-source project — not affiliated with or endorsed by Snapmaker.

beta.15 adds three features. Their most useful views appear **after you choose a file**
through the OS file picker, which can't be driven headlessly, so capture these manually
in the installed app (no private paths in frames):

## Plate Color Remap — visual preview
Open **Plate Color Remap**, choose a multicolor 3MF, pick a plate, then a source and
target colour. The preview shows the 2D plate map: one colour chip per object, the
changing object with a from→to swatch, painted/gold accents flagged with a lock, and a
legend with the source→target swap and untouched plates. Capture the wizard with a plate
selected and a from/to chosen.

## Print Quality Doctor — evidence
Open **Print Quality Doctor**, pick a symptom (e.g. "fails even with supports"), then
**Add your file**. Capture the result card showing the "What Studio found in your file"
panel with ok/warn/risk badges next to the general checks.

## Source Check
Open **Source Check**, choose an STL or 3MF. Capture the report: the detected source, the
"can read / cannot convert yet" lists, any risk, and the recommended next step. Good
examples: a U1 3MF (ready) and a non-U1 or PrusaSlicer file (prepare).

Carried-forward captures (Model Browser, Printer Hub, Project Doctor, Open in Orca) are
in the earlier SCREENSHOTS docs and the release pages.
